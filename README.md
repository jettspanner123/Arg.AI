# 🧠 Arg.AI — Learn Smarter Through AI Debates

**Arg.AI** is an intelligent learning platform where **two AI agents debate or discuss** a topic of your choice.  
You can **observe, summarize, and analyze** the debate to gain a deeper understanding of any subject.  
The app automatically provides **summarized insights, key takeaways, and a full discussion report** — helping users learn faster and more effectively.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🤖 **AI Debate Engine** — Two AI models debate on any topic selected by the user.  
- 🧩 **Smart Summarization** — Summarized and structured outcomes generated from debates.  
- 📄 **Full Report View** — Users can read the complete AI conversation or download it.  
- 🎯 **Topic Shortlisting** — Suggested debate topics for trending or relevant subjects.  
- 🧠 **Interactive Learning** — Learn through reasoning, contrast, and multi-perspective understanding.  
- 💾 **Persistent Storage** — Save and revisit your favorite AI debates anytime.
